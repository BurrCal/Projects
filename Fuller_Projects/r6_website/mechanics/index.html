Basic Game Mechanics

Movement

Use the W, A, S, and D keys to move your character forward, left, backward, and right, respectively. You can also sprint by holding down the Shift key.

Aiming and Shooting

Use your mouse to aim your weapon. Click the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Press the R key to reload.

Gadgets and Equipment

Rainbow Six Siege features a variety of gadgets and equipment that can help you complete objectives and defeat your enemies. Some examples include:

  • Breach charges
  • Smoke grenades
  • Flashbangs
  • C4
  • Drones
  • Thermite charges
  • Mirrors

Health and Damage

Your character has a certain amount of health that can be depleted by taking damage. If your health drops to zero, you will be "downed" and require assistance from a teammate to revive you. If you take additional damage while "downed," you will be "out of action" and unable to participate in the game until the next round.
